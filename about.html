The Keeper of This House

My life? Well... it's kind of like living in a haunted mansion. Shadows dance in the corners of my mind, strange stories echo through the halls, and you'll always find a little chill in the air around me — but that's just how I like it.

I'm a writer obsessed with the strange and spooky. When I'm not writing, I'm sketching the things that haunt my imagination — eerie faces, crumbling castles, dark forests, and creatures that watch from the shadows. My notebooks are like grim little museums of my nightmares... and my dreams.

And yes, I'm a gamer too — I dive into dark, mysterious worlds, chasing the thrill of the unknown and laughing at the jump scares along the way.
